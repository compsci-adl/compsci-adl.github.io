2018 Committee Nomination

Kaitie Bayly

I am a second year BCSA student.

Position(s)

  • President
  • Vice President
  • General Committee

What will you bring to the CS Club?

Unlike most programmers, I have a strong design background. This part of me offers a unique perspective and skill set that could come of use to the club. I will bring warmth, fun, events, encouragement and a comfortable, social club environment.

Why do you want to be on the committee?

I want to be on the committee because I enjoy socialising with like minded people. I intend to be someone that anyone and everyone can approach, whether that be for personal or CS related reasons. I want to ensure everyone receives the help or friend they need to really enjoy their time at uni.

What previous experience do you have that will help you serve the club?

I have served as Vice President and founder for another university club. I have experience tutoring CS subjects, and hence, experience helping and teaching peers. I have a Certificate IV in Design Fundamentals that has already landed me work with uni that combines both design and CS. These skills could replace the need to hire someone for pub crawl shirt design and other promotional material. Money saved from this could contribute to other CS related events.
