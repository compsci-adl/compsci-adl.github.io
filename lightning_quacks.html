Lightning Quacks

Sign Up!
What is it?

Lightning Quacks is a chance for you to show off to your peers. Every presenter gets 5 minutes to present on any topic of their choice. It could be computer science related, or it could be anything else. There'll be prizes, and cool people. and interesting talks. Oh, and free food.

The Details
  • Who? Everyone!
  • What? Give a 5 minute talk.
  • When? 17th of May, 2pm
  • Where? TBA
  • Why? Because it's cool, that's why.
The Prizes
  • Best Ducking Talk For the best talk we've seen today. Is well-presented, on an interesting topic, and engages the audience.
  • Golden Duckling For the best talk by a first year. Using the same criteria as Best Ducking Talk.
  • Biggest Quack Up For the funniest talk. Causing the judges to laugh out loud is a strong indicator.
  • Wing It For the best talk given by an unprepared presenter. We'll give you a topic on the day, and the chance to wing your way through.
  • Took a Quack For giving it a good solid effort. Basically judge's choice.
  • Like a Duck in Water For the most fluent, polished presentation. Slides are a must, demonstrations give extra points.
  • DUX For the best computer science related talk.
The Rules
  • You get 5 minutes. You may go under. You may not go over.
  • You must register. Registration closes 5 minutes before the first talk.
  • Don't be a jerk to presenters, even if you're just there to watch.
  • You can talk about anything. But again, don't be a jerk.
  • If you're being a jerk, we'll chuck you out, and you won't get free food.
  • Wing It Topics We'll allocate a topic. It won't require any technical knowledge.
